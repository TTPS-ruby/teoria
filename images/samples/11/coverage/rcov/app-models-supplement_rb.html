2 # == Summary
 
3 # A Supplement is a logical grouping of Editions and information that allows
 
4 # having a group of related objects linked to a specific Edition, invalidate
 
5 # another group of related objects without affecting other groups of objects.
 
6 #
 
7 # === Examples
 
8 # The supplement "Everyday news" has editions that invalidate each of
 
9 # them on a daily basis, while the supplement "Saturdays" has editions
 
10 # that appear on a weekly basis, every Saturday.
 
11 # By having the supplements group their editions, the ones associated to
 
12 # the "Everyday news" supplement won't invalidate the ones associated to
 
13 # the other supplement.
